# KrishiAI: Your Personal AI Agri-Scientist

KrishiAI is a revolutionary web application that places an AI agricultural scientist in every farmer's pocket. It provides instant, personalized advice on crop selection, pest detection via photo upload, real-time market prices, and hyper-local weather forecasts. By delivering critical information in regional languages with audio support, KrishiAI empowers farmers to increase yield, reduce risk, and boost profitability, making advanced agricultural knowledge accessible to all.

## Key Features

The application is built around four core modules, each designed to address the most pressing challenges faced by modern farmers:

**1. AI-Powered Crop Advisory:**
Farmers can ask specific questions in their native language about what to plant. The AI provides personalized recommendations based on crucial factors like the farmer's location and local soil type. To ensure maximum accessibility, all advice is delivered in both text and a clear audio format, making it easy to understand for everyone.

**2. Instant Pest & Disease Detection:**
One of the most powerful features is the ability to diagnose plant issues instantly. A farmer can simply upload a photo of an affected plant, and the AI will analyze it to identify the specific pest or disease. It provides a detailed diagnosis, a confidence score, and a set of actionable treatment options, all in the user's chosen language and available as audio.

**3. Real-Time Market Intelligence:**
KrishiAI connects farmers directly to market dynamics. It fetches current and historical price data for various crops from major agricultural markets. The platform displays price trends and provides strategic advice on both online and offline platforms where farmers can sell their produce, helping them secure the best possible prices.

**4. Hyper-Local Weather Forecasting & Alerts:**
The weather module delivers more than just a standard forecast. It provides hyper-local weather information, including temperature, wind speed, and humidity, along with a 7-day outlook. Most importantly, it generates predictive, actionable alerts for events like impending heavy rain, heatwaves, or strong winds, allowing farmers to take proactive measures to protect their crops. These alerts are also available in audio format for immediate comprehension.

## Benefits for Farmers

KrishiAI is more than just an app; it's a partner in farming. By providing data-driven insights, it helps reduce the risks associated with pests, weather, and market volatility. It democratizes agricultural knowledge, making expert advice that was once out of reach available to any farmer with a smartphone. This leads to healthier crops, higher yields, reduced waste, and ultimately, a more stable and profitable farming business.
